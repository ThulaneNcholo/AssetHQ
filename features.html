Features.

Application Management

Our application management feature streamlines the rental application process. Receive, review, and approve tenant applications all from one centralized location.

Owner Management

Our owner management feature allows you to easily manage the needs of property owners. Track financials, generate reports, and communicate with owners all from our platform.

Maintenance Request

Our maintenance request feature makes it easy for tenants to submit maintenance requests online. You can track requests, assign work orders, and manage the entire maintenance process in one place.

Tasks

Our task management feature lets you assign tasks to team members and vendors, track progress, and ensure timely completion of all tasks.

Property Inspections

Our property inspection feature lets you conduct property inspections, track maintenance needs, and ensure that your properties are up to code and in good condition.

Management Documents and Files

Our software lets you manage all of your management documents and files in one place. From lease agreements to vendor contracts, you can keep all important documents organized and easily accessible.

Tenant Management & Lease Agreements

Our tenant management feature lets you manage all aspects of your tenant relationships. From lease agreements to maintenance requests, you can keep track of everything in one place.

Our software allows you to easily manage lease agreements for all of your properties. Track renewal dates, manage move-ins and move-outs, and generate reports for all of your leases.

Management Financials

Our financial management feature lets you manage all aspects of your financials, including rent collections, expenses, income, and late payments. Our platform generates reports to help you make informed financial decisions.
